Research

Physics

Foundational physics and thermodynamics — resolving the Loschmidt paradox, relativistic thermodynamics, geometric entropy — alongside cosmology work on inflationary models and primordial power spectra.

Philosophy

Bayesian epistemology applied to longstanding philosophical problems, the demarcation of science, media polarization, and applied ethics. Argues that Bayesian frameworks dissolve rather than solve traditional debates.

Scientific Computation

GPU-accelerated Bayesian evidence calculation. SunBURST achieves O(d0.67) scaling through 1024+ dimensions, enabling model selection problems previously considered computationally impossible.

Misc.

AI consciousness ethics — Talmudic frameworks for responsible consciousness research and the "Suffering Toasters" methodology — and neuroscience work on ion channel dynamics and short-term plasticity.
